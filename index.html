Architecture

How WRAAS works

A four-stage pipeline continuously ingests, weighs, and approximates Romain's cognitive output in real time — with sub-200ms response latency.

01

Data Ingestion Layer

Continuously parses emails, commit messages, Slack threads, and calendar entries to maintain an up-to-date model of Romain's priorities and pet peeves.

02

Cognitive Weight Matrix

Each data point is assigned a weight based on recency, emotional intensity (measured in exclamation marks and emoji density), and topic relevance.

03

Opinion Approximation Engine

The core model generates responses calibrated to Romain's known positions: pragmatism over theory, outcomes over process, and always checking whether it runs in production.

04

Sigh Calibration & Output

Final responses are audited by the Exasperation Subsystem and delivered with the appropriate level of mild impatience or enthusiastic approval.
